DCDAQ - Expense Questions

Dependent Care Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur dependent care expenses?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Medical Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur medical care expenses?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Child Support and Alimony

·         Does anyone in the household pay child support or alimony?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

MAGI Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur expenses or deductions to be used for Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) for the household?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Shelter Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur shelter expenses such as rent, mortgage, etc. or receive a rent subsidy or live in public housing?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Utility Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur utility expenses such as heating, cooling, telephone etc.?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Other Expenses

·         Does anyone in the household incur other expenses such as educational expenses or impairment related work expenses (IRWE)?:   Select No, Pend or Yes.

Person Information

·         Person incurring expense:Displays the name of the individual incurring the expense.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Person incurring expense field is selected.

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date (CS/CD):  Enter the start or change date for a new circumstance. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         End Date:  This field is disabled and will display the final expense date if one is entered on this page.

·         Reported On:  Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Date Client Became Aware: Enter the date the client became aware of the change. Displays the Application File Datefor the Intake case action.

·         Verification:  Select the verification.

·         Final Expense Date: Enter the final expense date if the individual is no longer paying for dependent care.

·         Person for whom the expense is incurred:  Select the dependent for whom the expense is incurred.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Person for whom the expense is incurred field is selected.

·         Name:  Enter the individual’s name if the Person for whom the expense is incurred field is selected as “Person outside the household”.

Dependent Care / Frequency Amount

·         Frequency:  Select the pay expense frequency type.

·         Days per Month:Enter the days per month if the above field is Daily.

·         Amount per Frequency:  Enter the expense amount per frequency.

·         Verification:  Select the verification.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Provider in Household field is selected.
