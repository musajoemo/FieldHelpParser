DCIDD – Income Deduction – Details

This page captures details regarding each circumstance of income deductions for each individual on the case.

Person Information

·         Name:Displays the full name, age, and gender of the individual.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to the individual on the case.

Income Deduction Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date: (CS/CD) Enter the date on which the client’s income situation changes or begins. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         End Date:  Displays the situation end date. This field is disabled and will display the Final Payment Date if one is entered on this page.

·         Reported On:  Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Date Client Became Aware: Enter the date the client became aware of the change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Verification Received On:  Enterthe date the verification was received.

Income Deduction Information

·         Income Deduction Type:  Select the income deduction type if enabled.

·         Pay Frequency:  Select the pay frequency if enabled.

·         Final Pay Amount:  Enter the final pay amount.

·         Final Payment Date:  Enter the final payment date.

DCIDP - Income Deduction – Payments

Individual Information

·         Name:Displays the individual’s full name, age, and gender.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to the individual on the case.

Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date: (CS/CD) Enter the date on which the client’s income situation changes or begins. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         End Date:  Displays the end date for the situation.

Income Deduction Information

·         Income Deduction Type:  Displays the income deduction type.

·         Pay Frequency:  Displays the pay frequency.

Income Deduction Worksheet Information

·         Worksheet Start:  Enter the worksheet start date.

·         Worksheet End:  Enter the worksheet end date.

·         First Payment Date on or after:  Enter the first payment date that is on or after the first date of the worksheet start date.

·         Is this monthly payment always paid on the last day of the month?  Select Yesif Pay Frequency is Monthly. Otherwise, select No.

·         Second Payment Date:  Enter the second payment date if Pay Frequency is Semi-Monthly.

·         Is this second payment of the month always paid on the last day of the month?  Select Yes or No.

Income Deduction Worksheet Details

·         Income Date:  Displays the income deduction date.

·         Amount:  Enter the income deduction amount.

·         Actual/Anticipated:  Select Actual or Anticipated. Displays the past dates that are prior to the current date as Actual and displays future dates that are later than the current date as Anticipated.

·         Reason to exclude:  Select the reason for excluding the income deduction.

·         Verification:  Select the income deduction verification.

·         Did the client request their income to be averaged?  Select Yes or No.
