DCRQU - Resource Questions

This is the starting page for the Data Collection – Resources submodule. It contains a set of questions where you can navigate to a details page for a specific type of information.

 

If an item is recorded for any household member, the field displays Yes. If a record has an end date, the field displaysYes (Historical). If no records exist, the field displays No.

 If a response is Yes, the related details page for that question will display based on the case action. For example, if the question for liquid asset is Yes, the Liquid Asset Details page will display in the flow.

·         Does any household member own any resources?:Select Yes or No.

If Yes is selected, at least one resource question must be Yes. If this response is No, all resource questions are set to No.

Liquid Assets

·         Does anyone in the household own liquid assets such as cash-on-hand, stocks, bonds, checking or savings accounts, nursing home accounts, promissory notes, annuities, certificates of deposit, or retirement accounts?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Trust

·         Does anyone in the household own a trust?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Real Property/Contract for Deed

·         Does anyone in the household own any real property/contract for deed such as a home, mobile home/trailer, life estate, contract for deed?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Life Insurance

·         Does anyone in the household have life insurance?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Prepaid Funeral Agreement / Burial Plot

·         Does anyone in the household own a prepaid funeral agreement/burial plot?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Other Resource

·         Does anyone in the household own any other resource such as farm or business equipment or items of unusual value?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Livestock

·         Does anyone in the household own livestock?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Oil and Mineral Rights

·         Does anyone in the household own any oil and/or mineral rights?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Vehicle

·         Does anyone in the household own a vehicle such as a car, truck, motorcycle, camper, trailer, snowmobile, motor home, all terrain vehicle, watercraft/boat or aircraft?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Resource Transfer 36/60 Months

·         Has anyone given away or loaned money or property; sold or transferred real property or other resources within the last 36 months (or 60 months if a trust)?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

·         Resource Summary:  Click this button to open the Resource Summary view-only pop-up window.

DCRLQ - Liquid Asset – Details

This page captures liquid asset information owned by individuals in the case.

 

 Some fields on this page will display populated with information from prior actions.

Person Information

·         Name:Select the individual’s name if this is a new record.  Otherwise, displays the individual’s name.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Name field is selected.

Liquid Asset Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date:  (CS/CD) Enter the start or change date for a new circumstance. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

If you make a change to an existing record, you must change the CS/CD to establish the historical record. This will populate the end date on that historical record.

·         End Date:  This field is disabled and will display the final resource date if one is entered on this page.

·         Reported On:  Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Date Client Became Aware: Enter the date the client became aware of the change. Displays the Application File Datefor the Intake case action.

·         Verification Received On: Enter the date the client provided verification.

Liquid Asset Details

·         Liquid Asset Type:  Select the liquid asset type.

·         Financial Institution:  Enter the financial institution except if the above field is Cash.

·         Account Number:  Enter the account number if enabled.

·         Does the resource earn interest or dividends?:Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         Is Liquid Asset jointly owned?:Select Yes or No except if the Liquid Asset Type field is a Retirement Account.

·         Was this asset placed into a trust?:Select Yes or No.

·         Verification:  Select the verification.

·         End Reason:  Enter the end reason except for historical records.

·         Final Resource Date:  Enter the final resource date if an end reason is entered for the above field.

If you enter a final resource date, you must change the CS/CD to establish the historical record. This will populate the end date on that historical record.

Liquid Asset Amount Summary

·         Gross Value:  Enter the gross value amount.

·         Verification:  Select the verification for the above field.

·         Penalty Percentage for Early Withdrawal:  Enter the penalty percentage for early withdrawal if enabled.

·         Amount Owed:  Enter the amount owed if enabled.

·         Verification:  Select the verification if an amount is entered for the above field.

·         Net Value:  Displays the system-calculated net value which is the gross value minus the calculated penalty percentage and/or the amount owed. Displays 0 if the gross value is not provided or if the calculated penalty percentage and/or the amount owed is greater than the gross value.

Transfer

·         Was Liquid Resource transferred?:Select Yes or No.

Availability

·         Available?:Select Yes or No.

·         Availability Verification:  Select the verification if the above field is No.

·         History:   The History Previous and History Next buttons display enabled if historical records exist or disabled if no historical records exist.

·         Add Liquid Asset: Click this button to add a separate Liquid Asset entry.

DCRCD - Liquid Asset – Additional Details for Annuity and Promissory Note

This page captures additional information if a liquid asset was chosen as either an annuity or promissory note on the Liquid Asset – Details page.

 

 Some fields on this page will display populated with information from prior actions.

 This page is displayed on click of tab 'Additional Details', if 'Liquid Asset Type' is 'Annuity' or 'Promissory Note'.

Person Information

·         Name:Displays the individual’s full name, age and gender.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to the individual on the case.

Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date:  (CS/CD) Enter the start or change date for a new circumstance. Displays the CS/CD from the Liquid Assets – Details page.

If you make a change to an existing record, you must change the CS/CD to establish the historical record. This will populate the end date on that historical record.

·         End Date:  Enter the end date for the situation, if applicable.

Annuity

 The fields in this section are enabled when Annuity is selected as the Liquid Asset Type on the Liquid Asset – Details page.

·         Annuity Payment being received by:Select the individual(s) in the household who are receiving the annuity payment, including if there are others outside of the household. 

·         Characteristic:Select the characteristic.

·         Financial Institution:Enter the financial institution name.

·         Date Purchased/Converted:Enter the date the annuity was purchased or converted.

·         Fair Market Value:Enter the fair market value amount.

·         Annuity Verification:Select the annuity verification.

Promissory Note

 The fields in this section are enabled when Promissory Note is selected as the Liquid Asset Type on the Liquid Asset – Details page.

·         Characteristic:Select the characteristic.

·         Financial Institution:Enter the financial institution name.

·         Promissory Note Principal Balance:Enter the promissory note principal balance amount.

·         Date Payment Received:Enter the date payment was received.

·         Rebutted Value:Enter the rebutted value amount.

·         Lien Amount on Promissory Note:Enter the lien amount on the promissory note.

·         Equity:  Displays the equity value calculated as rebutted value minus lien amount on promissory note. If rebutted value is not available, but promissory note principal balance is available, equity value is calculated as promissory note principal balance minus lien amount.

·         Is this Promissory Note negotiable?:Select Yes or No.

·         Is this Promissory Note secured:Select Yes or No.

·         Secured By:Enter who secured the promissory note.

·         Secured Value:Enter the secured value amount.

·         Verification:Select the verification if enabled.

·         History:   The History Previous and History Next buttons display enabled if historical records exist or disabled if no historical records exist.

Dates

·         Reported On:  Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Verification Received On: Enter the date the client provided verification.

Transferred Asset Information

·         Asset Type:  Displays the asset type.

·         Description:  Enter specific information about the resource being transferred. For example, the address for a real property or the account number for an account.

·         Transferred Date:  Enter the date the resource was transferred.

·         Fair Market Value:  Enter the fair market value amount.

·         Verification:  Select the verification for the fair market value.

·         Value of Compensation Received:  Enter the value of compensation received amount.

·         Verification:  Select the verification of the compensation received.

·         Uncompensated Value:  Displays the uncompensated value amount calculated asthe fair market value minus the value of compensation received.

·         Is asset transfer to be excluded?:Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         Is this exclusion the result of a hearing or a transfer property hardship waiver?:  Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         Is transfer related to a trust?:  Select Yes or No.

·         Has any portion of the asset been returned?:  Select Yes or No.

 If a portion of a resource is returned, you need to add multiple resource records to account for this return. One record should account for the returned portion of the resource and then you need to add another record to account for the portion that wasn’t returned.

·         Date Transferred Back:  Enter the date the asset was transferred back.

·         Verification:  Select the verification for the asset transfer.

Transferred To

·         Name/Organization to whom asset is transferred:  Enter the name or organization to whom asset is transferred.

·         Nature of the transfer recipient?:Select the nature of the transfer recipient.

Policy states that the nature of the transfer recipient is selected and EDBC will use the appropriate allowance for DE MINIMIS. The nature of the transfer recipient values are:

­    A family member who is related by blood

­    A family member who is related by adoption

­    A family member who is related by existing marriage

­    A 501(c) non-profit organization

­    Other

·         Verification:  Select the verification for the nature of the transfer recipient.

Penalty Dates

·         Potential Health Coverage Penalty Begin Date:Enter the date that a penalty period should startif this transfer were to incur a penalty.

 Per policy, the Medicaid eligibility of an individual who transfers property on or after August 11, 1993, is governed by federal law. Section 1917(c) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396 p-C), as amended by OBRA-93 requires a penalty period of reduced Medicaid coverage for persons found to have made a property transfer for less than fair market value and for the purpose of becoming eligible for Medicaid. The transfer of property provision is applicable to applicants/recipients who are: Inpatients of nursing facilities or other medical institutions in which they are receiving equivalent nursing facility services; Receiving Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS). During a transfer of property penalty period, the recipient is ineligible for nursing facility services and home and community-based services. For more information about Transfer of Property rules, procedures and associated penalties, consult the Medicaid Eligibility Policy Manual at http://www.in.gov/fssa/ompp/4904.htm.

 To edit a record, click the field to enable the Edit function. Many Edit functions and Delete buttons are only viewable based on the worker’s security profile and role. Authorized workers with update capability should follow established policy and procedures to determine appropriate actions.
