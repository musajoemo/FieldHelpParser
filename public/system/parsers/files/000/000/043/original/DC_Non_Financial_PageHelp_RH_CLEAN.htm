DCNFQ-Non Financial - Questions

ABAWD

·         Are there any Able-Bodied Adults Without Dependents (ABAWD) mandatory individuals residing in the SNAP household?:Displays the value determined from EDBC.

Third Party Liability

·         Is anyone in the household covered by health insurance or potentially eligible for health insurance?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

·         Has anyone in the household had a work related illness/injury, a motor vehicle accident or other accidents/injuries in the last 24 months?:Select No, Pend or Yes. For Med Enrollment cases, displays No.

Other Program(s)

·         Applying for other program(s)?:Select No, Pend or Yes. For Med Enrollment cases, displays No and is disabled.

DCABB - ABAWD - Details

ABAWD Information

·         Name:Displays the individual’s full name, age and gender if the system determines that the individual is ABAWD.

·         Person ID:Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual.

·         36 Month ABAWD Clock Period:Displays the current ABAWD 36-month range. For example, a first range could begin 07/2015 and end 06/2018, then repeat every 36 months.

·         ABAWD Month:The first month will be displayed as the current calendar month plus 1.  All previous months up to the ABAWD period begin month will be displayed for an individual even if that individual is not an ABAWD for those months. Displays blank if not an ABAWD.

·         Status:Displays the previously saved status. Displays and enables only the current 36-month period on the first page. This field is disabled if the status is One-time three-month consecutive extension. EDBC cannot override this field.

Override By:Displays the currently logged-in worker and date if the Status was edited.Displays and enables only the current 36-month period on the first page. Once the 36 months are over, those months will be disabled.

DCABY - ABAWD - Summary

·         Name:Displays the individual’s full name, age and gender populated from EDBC.

·         Household Status:Displays In the Household or Out of Household, sorted with In the Household individuals first.

·         Status: Displays the status only for the current month for each ABAWD individual.  Displays Non-ABAWD if there is no status.

·         Date:Displays the monthof the last calculated ABAWD Status.

DCTPR - Third Party Liability Information - Details

Policy Holder Information

·         Policy Holder:Select the policy holder’s name if this is a new record.  Otherwise, displays the policy holder’s name.

·         Person ID:Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Policy Holder on the case is selected.

·         First:Enter the policy holder’s first name to add a new policy holder who is not on the case if enabled.

·         Middle:Enter the policy holder’s middle name if enabled.

·         Last:Enter the policy holder’s last name if enabled.

·         SSN:Enter the policy holder’s Social Security Number if enabled.

·         Date of Birth:Enter the policy holder’s date of birth if enabled.

Third Party Liability Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date (CS/CD):Enter the start or change date for a new circumstance. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         End Date: Enter an end date if the Third Party Liability is no longer applicable for the individual.

·         Reported On: Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Date Client Became Aware:Enter the date the client became aware of the change. Displays the Application File Datefor the Intake case action.

·         Verification Received On:Enter the date the client provided verification.

Insurance Company Information

·         Insurance Company:Displays the insurance company name after the Carrier # field below is completed.

·         Carrier #:Enter the insurance company’s Carrier number.

·         Phone #:Enter the insurance company’s phone number.

Policy Information

·         Policy #:Enter the policy number.

·         Group #:Enter the group number.

·         BIN#: Enter the BIN number

·         PCN: Enter the PCN

·         Policy Begin Date:Enter the begin date for the policy.

·         Policy End Date:Enter the end date for the policy.

·         Type of Coverage:Select the coverage type(s).

·         Verification:Select the verification.

Policy Premium Information

·         Is the premium deducted or could it be deducted from the policy holders wages/pension?:Select Yes or No.

·         Is this individual a qualified disabled worker?:Select Yes or No.

·         Is client paying?:Select Yes or No.

·         Begin Date of Medicare premium Part A:Enter the begin date ofthe Medicarepremium Part A plan if enabled.

·         Premium Amount:Enter the premium amount.

·         Does the policy holder need help obtaining/keeping coverage for self/family members?:Select Yes or No.

Policy Coverage Information

·         Verification:Select the verification.
