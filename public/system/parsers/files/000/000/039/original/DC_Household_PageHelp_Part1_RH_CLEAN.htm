DCINQ - Household Questions

Aged/Disability Benefits

·         Is there anyone in the case receiving aged/disability benefits such as SSI or RSDI?:  Select No, Pend or Yes.

Disability

·         Does anyone in the case claim to be disabled?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Out of State Benefit

·         In the last 60 days, has anyone in the household received Cash or SNAP benefits from another state or on a reservation?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Pregnancy

·         Is anyone in the case pregnant/postpartum?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Room and Board

·         Does anyone in the case pay for room and/or board?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Facility

·         Does anyone in the household reside in one of the following facility types: Hospital, Intermediate Care for Mental Retardation, Nursing Facility/Long Term Care, Psychiatric Hospital?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Medicare Claim

·         Is anyone in the case enrolled in Medicare Part A or B?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Minor Parent

·         Is there a minor parent in the case?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Special Medicaid Approval

·         Does anyone in the case have special Medicaid approvals (such as Home and Community BasedServices (Waiver), Breast and Cervical Cancer Program)?:Select No, Pend or Yes.

Medically Frail Status

·         Does anyone in the household have a Medically Frail Status?:Displays Yes when medcially frail information is received from the MCE CDEE Supplemental Interface, or a response is received from SSA.

DCSBI - Aged/Disability Benefits – Details

Person Information

·         Name:Select the individual’s name if this is a new record. Otherwise, displays the individual’s name.

·         Person ID:Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Name field is selected.

Aged/Disability Benefits Information Dates

·         Circumstances Start/Change Date:(CS/CD) Enter the start or change date for a new circumstance. Displays the first of the month, three months prior to the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         End Date: Enter the end date for the situation.

·         Reported On: Enter the date the client reported a change. Displays the Application File Date for Intake case action.

·         Date Client Became Aware:Enter the date the client became aware of the change. Displays the Application File Datefor the Intake case action.

·         Verification Received On:Enter the date the client provided verification.

Aged/Disability Benefits Assistance

·         Benefit Type:  Select the benefit type if enabled.

·         SSI Status:  Select the status if the above field is SSI.

·         Client applied for SSI/SNAP at SSA office:Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         Benefit Begin Date:  Enter the begin date if enabled.

·         Claim Number:Select the claim number.

·         Verification:  Select the verification.

·         SSI Living Arrangement:  Select the SSI living arrangement if the Benefit Type field is SSI.
