DCMSD - Social Summary – SSA Details

Social Summary Information

·         Name:Select the individual’s name if this is a new record. Otherwise, displays the individual’s name.

·         Person ID:Displays the unique ID number assigned to an individual. This field populates after the Name field is selected.

·         Social Summary Date:Enter the date if other than the current date.

·         Social Summary Type:Select Application or Progress Report if enabled.

Social Security Information

·         Does the applicant receive SSI or SSDI on the basis of disability?:Displays Yes if SSI is selected for the benefit type on the Aged/Disability Benefits – Details page. Otherwise, displays No.

·         If the applicant is not receiving SSI or SSDI on the basis of disability, has the applicant applied for Social Security Disability or Supplemental Security Income (SSI) Benefits?:Displays Yes or No based on the response for the “Has individual applied for SSI or SSDI?” question on the Disability – Details page. 

·         Date of Application:Displays the date of application entered on the Disability – Details page.

·         What is the status?:Displays the application status selected on the Disability – Details page.

·         Date of Denial:Displays the date of denial entered on the Disability – Details page.

·         If the applicant has not applied for SSA benefits due to disability, what is the reason?:Displays the response from this question on the Disability – Details page.

·         If the applicant has been denied SSA benefits, does he or she allege that since the denial, there is a new or different disability condition or the condition that was present during the SSA application has worsened?:Displays the response from this question on the Disability – Details page.

·         Explain:Enter the explanation if Yes is selected for the above field.

·         If Yes, has the applicant sought medical care for any or all of the condition(s) since the SSA denial?:Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         If No, give a reason:Enter the reason if enabled.

DCMSP - Social Summary – MRT Packet Tracking Checklist

The purpose of this pageis the following:

 

·         Aid with the steps necessary to collect information for the MRT Packet.

·         Collect dates associated with correspondence that will drive task creation for timeframes in the collection process.

·         Complete the MRT Packet process and submit the task to MRT for a formal disability determination.

Social Summary Tracking (Form 251B)

·         Name:Displays the individual’s full name, age and gender.

·         Person ID:  Displays the unique ID number assigned to the selected individual.

·         Social Summary:Displays the social summary date.

·         Social Summary Status:Displays Complete or In Progress based on the response for the Is Social Summary Complete? question on the Social Summary – Worker Observations page.  If Yes, the status displays as Complete. If No, the status displays In Progress.

·         Social Summary Type:Displays Application or Progress Report.

·         Is this an Accelerated Review?:Select Yes – List 1, Yes - List 2 or No.

·         Is this a waiver-approved case?:Select Yes or No.

Recent Exam Information

·         Has the client received a physical exam in the past 3 months?:Displays the selection for the Received Physical Exam in past 3 months? question on the Social Summary – Disability Treatment page.

·         Did the client request a 251a form to take to the Provider?:Select Yes or No.

·         1st Request: Date that 2320 (with optional 251a form or 3512 form) given or mailed to client:Enter the date.

·         Medical Provider and Exam date received from Client?:Select Yes or No.

·         Medical Info Form (2320/2321) Received Date:Enter the date.

·         2nd Request: Date that 2321 (with optional 251a form or 3512 form) sent to client:Displays the date populated by the system if the current date is greater than or equal to 1st Request: Date + 30 days and the Medical Provider and Exam date received from Client? question is No.

Provider Release Forms

·         Provider Name/Address:Displays the provider’s name and address for each Provider listed on the Social Summary – Medical Provider Summary page.

·         Obtained signed authorization for release form? (If No, give/mail 3512 form to client):Select Yes or No.

·         Date that authorization for release form was given or mailed to client:Enter the date.

Medical Records Collection

·         Provider Name/Address:Displays the provider’s name and address for each provider for which the client has a signed 3512 form.

·         1st Letter Sent – 2322/251a or 3512 (to Provider):Enter the date.

·         Received Medical Records from Provider?:Select Yes or No.

·         2nd Letter Sent – 2323/251a or 3512 (to Provider and Client/AR):Enter the date if enabled.

·         Contact Provider for possible extension. Did Provider request extension?:Select Yes or No if enabled.

·         If Provider does not intend to reply – Unable to obtain records from Provider – 3rd Letter Sent – 2324 (to Client):Displays the date populated by the system if the above field is No.

Medical Records Collection – Extension Requested

·         Provider Name/Address:Displays the provider’s name and address for each provider who answered Yes to the Did Provider request extension? question.

·         3rd Letter Sent – 2323/251a or 3512 (to Provider):  Enter the date. 

·         Received Medical Records from Provider within 20-day extension?:Select Yes or No.

·         Unable to obtain records from Provider – 4th Letter Sent – 2324 (to Client):Enter the date if the above field is No.

MRT Activity and Notifications

·         Application was denied by worker:Select Yes or No.

·         Date Sent to MRT:Displays the current date.

·         Packet Status:Select All Medical Records Received or Partial Packet.

·         Additional Information Requested from MRT:Displays any additional information from the MRT interface.
